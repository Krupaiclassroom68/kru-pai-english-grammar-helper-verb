🎯 What do you practice?

This game helps students practice helper verbs: is, am, are, do, does, did, has, have in real sentence patterns.

🕹️ Stage 1

Read the sentence and tap the correct helper verb. Each correct answer gives +1.

✏️ Stage 2

Choose Correct or Change. If the sentence is wrong, fix it with the correct helper verb.

🔥 Bonus & Pass Rule

Every 5 correct in a row gives +2 bonus. Accuracy 80%+ unlocks the celebration certificate.

💡 Goal: train children to notice the subject, tense clue, and helper verb pattern quickly and accurately.
